### Exponents

$$
\begin{aligned}

X^AX^B  &= X^{A+B} \\
\frac{X^A}{X^B} &=X^{A-B} \\
(X^A)^B&=X^{AB} \\
X^N+X^N&=X{2N} \\
2^N+2^N&=2^{N+1}

\end{aligned}

$$

### Logarithms

In computer science, all logarithms are to base 2 unless specified otherwise.

DEFINITION: x^a = b if and only if $ \log_x{b} = a $

$$
\begin{aligned}

\log_A({B}) &=\frac{\log_C{A}}{\log_B{A}}; \space C>0 \\
\log{AB} &=\log{A}+\log{B}

\end{aligned}

$$
